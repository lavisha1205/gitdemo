Laundry Care Tips

  • Separate white and colored clothes before washing.
  • Always check garment care labels.
  • Avoid overloading the washing machine.
  • Remove stains as early as possible.
  • Store clothes in a clean and dry place.
  • Use suitable detergents for delicate fabrics.
